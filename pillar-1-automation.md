# Image prompt — Pillar 1: Automation

**Filename when saved:** `assets/pillar-automation.png`
**Aspect ratio:** 16:9 (landscape)
**Recommended model:** `gemini-2.5-flash-image-preview` (handles style transfer from a reference image and avoids stray text artifacts). Imagen 4 will work but tends to produce more 3D-rendered output.
**Reference image to attach:** `assets/paper-robots.png` (the existing brand paper-craft hero)

---

## Prompt

Match the style of the reference image EXACTLY: layered paper-craft sculpture, hand-cut paper with visible folds and crisp edges, deep shadows from layered paper, indigo and azure swirling paper waves in the background, peach/ochre confetti flecks, off-white textured paper, soft studio lighting.

**Subject — AUTOMATION:** A small friendly cream-and-blue paper-craft robot character (similar build and personality to the small robot in the reference) stepping back from a folded-paper conveyor belt or pipeline. Paper-cut documents, envelopes, and small geometric shapes (diamonds, dots, squiggles in indigo / azure / peach paper) flow along the belt on their own. The robot watches, hands at its sides, hands-off — confident, not anxious.

**Background:** Layered paper waves and curved ribbons in deep indigo (#2E1B88), azure (#1479E1), and violet (#4E1B61). Cream / off-white paper accents. A few small confetti-like peach / ochre (#E17A14) paper flecks for warmth.

**Composition:** Wide 16:9, soft studio lighting from the upper-left, sharp paper edges and visible folds, clear depth from layered paper.

**Hard constraints:**
- NO text, logos, numbers, labels, or words anywhere
- Pure paper-craft sculpture aesthetic — NOT 3D rendered, NOT photorealistic, MUST look like layered cut paper
- Robot must read as a paper construction (visible folds, paper texture), not a sleek 3D character

---

## Why this image

This is the visual for **Pillar 1 — "Work happens without you."** The robot stepping back captures "autonomous workflows that do the manual stuff." The conveyor belt visualises the pipeline. Documents flowing on their own = the work happening without human input.
