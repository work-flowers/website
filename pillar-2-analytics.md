## Prompt

Match the style of the reference image EXACTLY: layered paper-craft sculpture, hand-cut paper with visible folds and crisp edges, deep shadows from layered paper, indigo and azure swirling paper waves in the background, peach/ochre confetti flecks, off-white textured paper, soft studio lighting.

**Subject — ANALYTICS:** A scene of paper-cut bar charts of varying heights (made of folded indigo, azure, and violet paper), with a single rising line graph formed by a curling azure paper ribbon arching up and to the right above them. Paper-cut pie wedges and small data-point dots scatter around the scene. A small cream-and-blue paper-craft robot or figure (geometric, like the robot in the reference) gestures toward the rising line — engaged but unhurried.

**Background:** Layered paper waves in deep indigo (#2E1B88), azure (#1479E1), and violet (#4E1B61), with off-white paper plane. Peach / ochre (#E17A14) confetti flecks.

**Composition:** Wide 16:9, soft studio lighting from the upper-left, sharp paper edges and visible folds, clear depth from layered paper.

**Hard constraints:**
- ABSOLUTELY NO text, numbers, words, axis labels, chart titles, or any rendered typography — pure abstract shapes only
- Pure paper-craft sculpture aesthetic — NOT 3D rendered, NOT photorealistic, MUST look like layered cut paper
- The chart must read as paper, not as a UI screenshot

## Why this image

This is the visual for **Pillar 2 — "Work you can see."** The bar charts and rising line ribbon make "dashboards, warehouses, metrics that matter" tangible. The friendly figure gesturing toward the chart suggests partnership — we ship insights, you understand them.
