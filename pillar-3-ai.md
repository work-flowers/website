# Image prompt — Pillar 3: AI Enablement

**Filename when saved:** `assets/pillar-ai.png`
**Aspect ratio:** 16:9 (landscape)
**Recommended model:** `gemini-2.5-flash-image-preview` (handles style transfer from a reference image and avoids stray text artifacts). Imagen 4 will work but tends to produce more photorealistic output.

## Prompt

Match the style of the reference image EXACTLY: layered paper-craft sculpture, hand-cut paper with visible folds and crisp edges, deep shadows from layered paper, indigo and azure swirling paper waves in the background, peach/ochre confetti flecks, off-white textured paper, soft studio lighting.

**Subject — AI ENABLEMENT:** A small cream-and-blue paper-craft human figure (simple geometric paper construction like the robot in the reference) standing centered, with a glowing paper-cut "spark" or starburst above their open hand — made of layered paper rays in azure and peach. Around the figure, paper-cut tools fan out: a paper chat-bubble with three dots, a paper folded document with a corner-fold, a paper email envelope, a paper bar-chart fragment — all interconnected by curling paper-ribbon threads in azure (#1479E1) and peach. The threads suggest an AI assistant orchestrating between tools.

**Background:** Layered paper waves in deep indigo (#2E1B88), azure (#1479E1), and violet (#4E1B61), with off-white paper accents. Peach / ochre (#E17A14) confetti flecks.

**Composition:** Wide 16:9, soft studio lighting from the upper-left, sharp paper edges and visible folds, clear depth from layered paper.

**Hard constraints:**
- NO text, logos, numbers, or words anywhere — the chat bubble has only abstract dots, the document has paper "lines" not letters, the envelope is blank
- Pure paper-craft sculpture aesthetic — NOT 3D rendered, NOT photorealistic, MUST look like layered cut paper
- The figure should feel friendly and creative, not corporate

## Why this image

This is the visual for **Pillar 3 — "Your team, with AI superpowers."** The figure with a glowing spark = a person made more capable by AI. The orchestration of tools (chat / docs / email / charts) connected by ribbons = "AI inside the tools you already use." Custom Skills, MCP, integrations — abstracted into a single paper-craft moment.
